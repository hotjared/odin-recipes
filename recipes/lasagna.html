Lasagna

Description

Layer upon layer of thin, tender sheets of pasta interwoven with rich, velvety béchamel sauce, vibrant marinara, and a hearty medley of seasoned ground meat, sautéed onions, garlic, and finely chopped vegetables, harmonizing in a symphony of flavors. Each bite unveils a savory embrace of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, cascading over the palate like a comforting blanket of indulgence. This Italian classic, baked to golden perfection, transcends mere sustenance, embodying a culinary masterpiece that delights the senses with every luscious, decadent forkful.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Marinara sauce
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Gather lasagna noodles, marinara sauce, béchamel sauce, ground beef or Italian sausage, and mozzarella cheese. Cook the meat with onions, garlic, and seasoning until browned.
  2. Boil Noodles: Cook lasagna noodles until al dente according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Layer Lasagna: Preheat oven. In a baking dish, layer marinara sauce, noodles, béchamel sauce, cooked meat, and mozzarella. Repeat until ingredients are used, ending with cheese on top.
  4. Bake Lasagna: Cover dish with foil and bake for 30-40 minutes until cheese is bubbly. Remove foil for the last 10 minutes to brown cheese.
  5. Server and Enjoy: Let cool slightly, then slice and serve. Garnish with herbs if desired. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
